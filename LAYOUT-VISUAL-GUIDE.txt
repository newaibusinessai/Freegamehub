                            AD UNIT SUMMARY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📊 DESKTOP (> 1200px):
   ✅ Left Sidebar:  2 ad units (160x600 + 160x250)
   ✅ Right Sidebar: 2 ad units (160x600 + 160x250)
   ✅ Total:         4 ad units per page
   ✅ Behavior:      Sticky (follows scroll)

   ✅ Content:       Full width
   ✅ Total ads:     0 (can add mobile banners if needed)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                          ADVANTAGES OF THIS LAYOUT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ More Ad Revenue:
   - 4 ad units instead of 2-3 banner ads
   - Sticky ads = always visible = more impressions
   - Higher viewability = better CPM rates

✅ Better User Experience:
   - Content area stays clean and uncluttered
   - No ads interrupting game play
   - Ads feel less intrusive than banners
   - Mobile users get ad-free experience

✅ Professional Look:
   - Matches layout of successful game sites
   - Similar to Solitaire Bliss and CardGames.io
   - Clean, modern design

✅ SEO Benefits:
   - Content stays "above the fold"
   - Better mobile experience = better rankings
   - Faster page load (fewer ad units on mobile)
